Chicken and Dumplings instructions

  1. fry chicken fat side down, about 7 minutes each side.
  2. fry in two batches, using 2 tbsp oil each batch.
  3. remove chicken and skin.
  4. add carrots and celery and saute until soft.
  5. add salt and flour.
  6. whisk in sherry.
  7. stir in stock, thyme, milk and bay leaves.
  8. return chicken, cook 1hr.
  9. remove and shred chicken.
  10. add shredded chicken back to stew.
  11. add frozen peas and parsley.
  12. microwave milk and fat for 1 minute.
  13. mix in flour and salt and baking powder.
  14. add some parsley.
  15. drop golf ball size dough balls on top of stew.
  16. cook approximately 18 minutes.
