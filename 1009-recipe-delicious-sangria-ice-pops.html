Ingredients of Sangria Ice Pops

  1. It's of Ice Pops.
  2. You need 750 ml of fruity red wine.
  3. You need 2 each of plums (pitted & chopped).
  4. Prepare 1 large of granny Smith apple (chopped).
  5. Prepare 1 large of ripe pear (chopped).
  6. Prepare 1/3 cup of water.
  7. Prepare 1/3 cup of sugar.
  8. It's 1 each of orange (unpeeled & chopped).
  9. You need 1 cup of unsweetened pomegranate juice.
  10. It's 1/4 cup of fresh orange juice.
  11. You need 2 tbsp of light corn syrup.

Sangria Ice Pops instructions

  1. In 4-quart saucepan, boil wine, plums, apples & pear on high 12-15 min or until reduced by half..
  2. In 1-quart pan, boil water & sugar on high 2 min or until sugar dissolves. Cool..
  3. Transfer wine mixture to large bowl; stir in orange, juices, sugar syrup & corn syrup. Cover; refrigerate 4 hrs or up to 2 days..
  4. Place ice-pop molds in freezer 1 hr before filling..
  5. Pour fruit mixture through strainer set over pitcher; discard solids. Pour liquid into molds, leaving 1/4 in at the top of each; freeze 4 hrs or until solid..
  6. Dip molds in lukewarm water to loosen ice Pops before pulling them out..
