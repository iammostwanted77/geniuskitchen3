Garlic chicken parmesan instructions

  1. Hammer the chicken breasts to make them flat.
  2. Marinate the chicken breasts with 1/2 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp white pepper powder, Worcestershire sauce, mustard paste for at least half and hour or can be kept over night..
  3. On a grilling pan grill chicken from both sides until cooked and set aside..
  4. In a pan add 1 tsp olive oil and saute mushrooms and spinach until soft. Set aside..
  5. Heat olive oil and saute garlic. Add flour and saute. Add chicken stock and cream n mix well. Add everything except cheese n mix. Add cheese in the end n serve with steamed vegetables or over pasta..
