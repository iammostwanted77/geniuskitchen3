  3. Add the purée to the pan and throw away any remaining pulp is left in the sieve. Add the parsley, basil, sugar and tomato purée. Mix well.
  4. Add the stock and water, stir and bring to the boil. Turn the heat down to a simmer and continue cooking with the lid on for 30 minutes. Remove the lid then continued simmering until the sauce is thick. Season with salt and pepper then set aside..
