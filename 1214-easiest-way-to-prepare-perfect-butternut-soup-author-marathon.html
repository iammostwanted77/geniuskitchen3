Butternut soup# author Marathon instructions

  1. Peel your butter nut..
  2. Cut into large pieces and wash clean..
  3. Heat oil in a pan and fry in onions till golden brown..
  4. Add in the sliced butter nut pieces, salt, water and cover..
  5. Once cooked add ginger and milk then blend..
  6. Serve as you wish, with breads or plain. Enjoy.
