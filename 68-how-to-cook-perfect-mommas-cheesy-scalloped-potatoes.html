Ingredients of mommas cheesy scalloped potatoes

  1. Prepare 1 medium of onion.
  2. It's 6 medium of potatoes.
  3. Prepare 3 tbsp of butter.
  4. You need 1 dash of pepper.
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp of salt or season salt.
  6. It's 1/2 cup of sour cream.
  7. It's 3/4 cup of milk.
  8. Prepare 1/3 cup of flour.
  9. It's 1/2 cup of cheese of your choice.

mommas cheesy scalloped potatoes instructions

  1. preheat oven to 375.
  2. dice up onion.
  3. slice potatoes or cube them.
  4. add onion and butter to saucepan and simmer.
  5. add flour to simmering onions.
  6. add milk, salt, and pepper to onion and flour mix.
  7. add sour cream and and wisk.
  8. in a casserole pan covered in non stick spray place a layer of sliced or cubed potatoes.
  9. pour half sauce mixture over layer of potatoes.
  10. add another layer of potatoes.
  11. cover with remaining sauce.
  12. cover pan with foil..
  13. bake for 45 minutes.
  14. remove foil and add cheese.
  15. bake uncovered for another 15 minutes.
