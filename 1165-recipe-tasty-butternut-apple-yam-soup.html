Ingredients of Butternut-Apple-Yam soup

  1. It's 1/2 lb of butter.
  2. You need 4 cup of chopped onion.
  3. Prepare 1 tsp of ground ginger.
  4. You need 4 lb of peeled, seeded, chopped into 1 inch cubes, butternut squash.
  5. Prepare 2 lb of peeled, chopped yams.
  6. Prepare 2 lb of cored, chopped apples.
  7. It's 1/2 cup of brown rice.
  8. Prepare 12 cup of stock.
  9. It's 2 tsp of salt.
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp of black pepper.
  11. It's 4 tsp of curry powder.

Butternut-Apple-Yam soup instructions

  1. preheat oven to 400°F. roast squash, yams and apples on papered sheet trays until softened..
  2. melt butter in a skillet, saute onions until translucent but not yet browning. sprinkle in curry and ginger, mix well. saute another 5 minutes, transfer to soup pot..
  3. transfer squash, etc. to pot. add stock, salt and pepper. cover and simmer one hour..
  4. in batches, puree mixture in a blender or food processor until smooth..
