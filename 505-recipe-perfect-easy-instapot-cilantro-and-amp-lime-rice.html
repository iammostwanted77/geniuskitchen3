Ingredients of Easy instapot Cilantro & Lime Rice

  1. You need 2 cups of rice.
  2. It's 2 cups of water.
  3. It's 1/4 cup of chopped fresh cilantro.
  4. You need 1/8 cup of lime juice.
  5. It's 1/2 Tsp of salt.
  6. It's 1/2 Tsp of pepper.

Easy instapot Cilantro & Lime Rice instructions

  1. Add two cups of water into instapot. Add in lime juice,salt,pepper, and cilantro..
  2. Stir lime juice, cilantro, spices, and water until it looks well incorporated..
  3. Add in two cups of rice. Make sure grains are completely covered by liquid. Cover with instapot lid and set on regular white rice setting..
  4. Enjoy!.
