  1. It's 1 lb of ground beef.
  2. It's 1 can of biscuits.
  3. Prepare 1 can of pork n beans.
  4. It's 1/2 cup of ketchup.
  5. It's 1 packages of shredded cheddar cheese.

redneck shepards pie step by step

  1. place all your biscuits in a pie pan and press down a bit to form your base.
  2. brown your beef and drain.
  3. to your beef add the ketchup and beans.
  4. layer the mixture and the cheese on top of your biscuits.
  5. bake at required temp on your biscuit can plus five mins.
  6. serve!!.
