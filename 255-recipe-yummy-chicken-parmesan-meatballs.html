Chicken Parmesan Meatballs instructions

  1. Preheat oven 375 degress.
  2. Spray muffin pam w/ cooking spray.
  3. Cut the mozzarella into 1" cubes.
  4. Stir together 1/4 cup breadcrumbs,onion, parsley, parmesan, salt, pepper,garlic and egg.
  5. Add ground chicken.. wet hands to mix.
  6. Form balls the size of the palm, press cheese in the middle cover cheese with meat.
  7. Roll the meatballs in the rest of the breadcrumbs.
  8. place meatballs in tin.
  9. Bake for 20-25 mins, until cooked all the way through.
  10. Top with sauce.
