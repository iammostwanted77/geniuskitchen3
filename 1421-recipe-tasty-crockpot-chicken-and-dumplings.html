Ingredients of Crockpot Chicken and Dumplings

  1. It's 300 g of skinless, boneless chicken breasts cut into 1 inch pieces.
  2. It's 12 of good quality potatoes, cut into 1 inch pieces.
  3. Prepare 135 g of whole baby carrots.
  4. Prepare 1 stalk of celery sliced.
  5. Prepare 1 can (300 g) of condensed cream of chicken soup.
  6. It's 120 mL of water.
  7. It's 1 teaspoon of dried thyme leaves, crushed.
  8. Prepare to taste of Ground black pepper.
  9. You need 800 mL of milk.

Crockpot Chicken and Dumplings step by step

  1. Place the chicken, potatoes, carrots and celery into the insert of the crockpot.
  2. Stir the soup, water, thyme and black pepper into a small bowl. Mix well and pour the mixture over the chicken and vegetables.
  3. Cover and cook on low 7 to 8 hours or until the chicken is cooked through.
