Ingredients of Creamy Lime N Cilantro Dressing

  1. It's 1 cup of mayo.
  2. You need 1 cup of greek yogurt (i used plain but u can play with different flavours).
  3. You need 4 tbsp of red wine vinegar.
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp of honey.
  5. Prepare 1 of medium lime, juice freshly squeezed.
  6. It's 5 tbsp of fresh cilantro chopped.
  7. It's 1 tsp of ground black pepper.
  8. Prepare 1 clove of (small-medium) garlic minced.
  9. You need 1 tsp of salad spice* (if you don't have this see direction 5).

Creamy Lime N Cilantro Dressing instructions

  1. Whisk honey, red wine vinegar and lime juice together in a small bowl (altogether makes about 1/2 cup of mixture).
  2. In a separate container mix the mayo and Greek yogurt, add the lime mixture and fold in until well blended, fold in cilantro, spices and garlic, refrigerate.
  3. Variations: different yogurts, cream cheeses, various fresh herbs ( ex rosemary or basil or both! Parsley etc...), sundried tomatoes, green olives, kalamata olives, bacon bits, green onions, different juices (orange, strawberry, lemon) etc etc..
  4. Serve with any type of salad, so far I've made this recipe for a tomato and yellow pepper chopped salad and for a creamy macaroni salad, they both turned out addictive! This recipe makes ~ 2.5 cups of dressing, use ~1.5 tbsp/serving. Can be kept well in fridge for several days in a sealed container..
