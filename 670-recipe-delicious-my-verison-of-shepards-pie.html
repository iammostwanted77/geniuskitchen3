My verison of shepards pie instructions

  1. Brown ur ground beef and onion together.
  2. After meat is browned drain fat and add seasoning and gravy packet.
  3. After adding season & gravy follow instructions on gravy packet.
  4. Once meat is done turn to low and make potatoes feel free to take the time to make real potatoes i found that these work fine i always add a little milk because they come out a little to thick for my liking.
  5. While waitimg for water to boil grate ur cheddar chesse.
  6. Once potatoes are done start layering ur ingredients in appropriate size cassorole dish i ise corn u can use peas and carrot or corn peas and carrots.
  7. Top with cheddar chesse and put in 350 degree oven for about 20 mins so chesse is melted and everything heated through let cool for 5-10 mins serve and enjoy.
