Ingredients of mushroom chicken parmesan

  1. You need 4 of chicken breasts.
  2. It's 1/4 cup of melted butter.
  3. You need 2/3 cup of flour for coating.
  4. Prepare 2/3 cup of half and half.
  5. Prepare 5 oz of fresh sliced mushrooms.
  6. You need 1/3 cup of parmesan cheese grated.

mushroom chicken parmesan instructions

  1. preheat oven 350.
  2. rinse dry chicken and dip in melted butter then dredge in flour.
  3. place in baking dish cover in cream.
  4. top with mushrooms and cheese.
  5. bake 30 to 45 minutes and cool for 10 minutes.
