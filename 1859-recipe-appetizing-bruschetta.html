Bruschetta step by step

  1. chop tomatoes and basil leaves. Add olive oil, balsamic oil and spices. place in refrigerator to chill..
  2. Slice bread in 1/2 inch slices ln an angle. place on cooking sheet, sprinkle with olive oil. Toast in oven until slightly crispy..
  3. place bread ln plate, put cheese on bread, spoon on tomato topping. Serve..
