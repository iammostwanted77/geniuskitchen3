my moms shepards pie instructions

  1. pre heat your oven to 375.
  2. in a large skillet fry up the meat and onion togther.
  3. in a large pot cut and boil the potatos I always leave the skins on them.
  4. when meat is done set aside.
  5. when taters are done drain and mash to your liking but you will want them a little thick add what ever you like to add to your normal mash taters.
  6. in a Large baking dish scoop some taters into the pan just enuf to cover the sides and bottom of pan.
  7. pot a layer of cheese down.
  8. put a layer or your meat mixture.
  9. put a layer of your corn.
  10. keep going like this till you run out of space.
  11. the taters should always end up on top as the last layer.
  12. then take the rest of your cheese and spread it out on top.
  13. bake untill cheese is golden brown.
