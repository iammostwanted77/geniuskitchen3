Ingredients of Spicy Butternut Soup

  1. Prepare 1 of chopped onion.
  2. It's 2 of tomatoes peeled and seeded.
  3. Prepare 1 of Butternut chunked.
  4. Prepare 3 of garlick cloves chopped.
  5. You need 2 of Vegetable stock cubes.
  6. It's 1 tsp of Mild Curry Powder.
  7. You need 125 grams of Butter.
  8. It's 1 of Salt & Pepper to taste.
  9. You need 2 liter of Water.

  2. Saute onions & garlick until soft..
  3. Add rest of the ingredients. Bring soup to the boil..
  4. Boil soup for 30minutes. Remove from heat and liquidize..
  5. Adjust seasoning to taste..
