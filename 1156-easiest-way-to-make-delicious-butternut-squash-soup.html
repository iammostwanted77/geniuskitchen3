Butternut Squash Soup instructions

  1. Preheat oven 400Â°F.
  2. Rub squash with 2 tablespoons of oil.
  3. place in shallow dish.
  4. bake for one hour then set aside to cool.
  5. slice squash in half.
  6. using spoon take out seeds and remove skin.
  7. put starchy flesh in bowl and smash well.
  8. heat remaining oil in pan.
  9. add onions and cook until translucent.
  10. add squash to onions and cook for a couple of minutes.
  11. add broth to squash, bring to boil, then reduce heat to simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  12. put mixture into blender and blend until smooth.
  13. transfer soup to pan and add salt, pepper, and vanilla.
  14. stir well.
  15. put in bowls and add cilantro over each bowl..
  16. add avocado if desire.
