Ingredients of Loaded chicken and dumplings EASY

  1. Prepare 2 of chicken breasts.
  2. It's 1 (32 ounce) of box of chicken broth.
  3. Prepare 2 cups of water.
  4. It's 1 packet of French onion soup mix.
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon of garlic powder.
  6. It's 1 teaspoon of onion powder.
  7. You need 2 packets of Sazon.
  8. You need 1 tablespoon of Italian seasoning.
  9. It's 1/4 teaspoon of seasoning salt.
  10. It's 1/4 teaspoon of pepper.
  11. It's 1 cup of tofu diced into 1/4 inch pieces.
  12. You need 2 cups of mushrooms diced.
  13. You need 1/2 cup of diced onion, I used vidalia onions.
  14. Prepare 1 can of corn.
  15. It's 1 cup of carrots diced small.
  16. It's 1 packet of pillsbury biscuits.

Loaded chicken and dumplings EASY instructions

  1. Place water, chicken,onions, carrots, chicken broth, dry spices and herbs, and French onion soup mix in a kettle and cook about an hour on medium heat or until carrots are soft..
  2. Remove chicken and shred..
  3. Place shredded chicken back in kettle and add mushrooms and tofu. Cook 10 minutes more. Add corn..
  4. Bring kettle to a rolling boil and break each biscuit into about 8-10 small pieces and roll into balls. Place in kettle and boil 10 minutes. Let cool 15-20 minutes and serve!.
