Cheats Quik'n"easy Creme Brulee step by step

  1. In a 2 cup capacity measuring jug beat the eggs.
  2. Add the sugar and mix well.
  3. Add vanilla extract/essence (anywhere from 2 drops to a splash depending on your taste).
  4. Add milk and mix well.
  5. Pour into small ramikin dishes.
  6. Stand ramikins in a flat based microwavesafe tray and pour water around them in the tray (depth about 1/3 of way up sides or to first finger knuckle).
  7. Place in microwave and cook on high for 4 minutes.
  8. Turn on grill to heat.
  9. Carefully (it's hottttt!!!!) remove ramikins and sprinkle a layer of brown sugar and place under hot grill just until sugar melts and just starts to crisp.
  10. Serve.
