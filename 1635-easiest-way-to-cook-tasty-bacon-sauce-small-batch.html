Ingredients of Bacon Sauce Small Batch

  1. Prepare 3 slices of smoked bacon.
  2. It's 2 tablespoon of sugar.
  3. You need 1/2 teaspoon of molasses.
  4. Prepare 1 of medium onion.
  5. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
  6. You need 1 tablespoon of butter.
  7. It's 1 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil.
  8. It's To taste of ground black pepper.
  9. You need Pinch of cayenne pepper powder.
  10. You need 1 tablespoon of water.
  11. You need 2 tablespoons of sherry.
  12. Prepare 1 tablespoon of apple cider vinegar.
  13. Prepare 1 teaspoon of concentrated tomato paste.

Bacon Sauce Small Batch step by step

  1. Fry the bacon in a pan reserving 1 tablespoon bacon drippings. Set bacon aside and keep the rest of the harvested bacon drippings for another recipe. Dice the onion. Add the butter and olive oil to the pan, with the drippings..
  2. Add onion and sauté till translucent. Mix the sugar and molasses. Chop the bacon into small pieces..
  3. Add all the ingredients to the onions, except the apple cider vinegar. Sauté for 10 minutes..
  4. Add the apple cider vinegar stirring often till it's thick as you like. Serve as a condiment or as an add in sauce. I hope you enjoy!!!.
