Roasted Butternut, Sweet Potato and Carrot soup instructions

  1. preheat oven to gas mark 6.
  2. chop butternut into cubes and deseed place on roasting tray, do the same for potatoes and carrots. I don't peel as adds taste.
  3. pour over olive oil and place in oven for 30 to 40 minutes or gone roasted colour..
  4. boil kettle and pour out into jug half a pint add Vegas stock cube and stir.
  5. place chopped onion and garlic cloves into soup maker or blender.
  6. add Veg stock, salt and pepper, basil and corriander.
  7. now check roasted veg if done to a light crispy colour or to your own tastes remove from oven and place in soup maker or blender.
  8. turn on soup maker select program wait and lovely hot soup.
  9. if using blender, bend then put one serving into pan and heat gently.
