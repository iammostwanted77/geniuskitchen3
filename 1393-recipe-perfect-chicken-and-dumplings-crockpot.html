Ingredients of Chicken And Dumplings - Crockpot

  1. Prepare 4 each of Chicken (Boneless & Skinless).
  2. You need 2 tbsp of Butter.
  3. It's 2 can of Cream of Chicken Soup (10.75 oz).
  4. It's 1 each of Onion (Small & Diced finely).
  5. Prepare 2 packages of Refrigerator Biscuits (torn into small pieces).

Chicken And Dumplings - Crockpot step by step

  1. Place chicken, soup, and onion in slow cooker and fill with enough water to cover..
  2. Cover and cook 5-6 hours on high..
  3. 30-45 minutes before serving, add biscuits pieces to pot and cook until dough is not raw..
