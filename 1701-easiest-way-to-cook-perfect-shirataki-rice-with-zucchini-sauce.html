Shirataki rice with zucchini sauce step by step

  1. Boil in a pot water for the rice..
  2. Chop very small pieces of the zucchini, onion and tomato and put it in a pan in medium fire and let it cook. Add half glass of light vegetable broth,salt, pepper and stir..
  3. When the water is boiled in the pot add the rice and cook it in medium fire for 10 min..
  4. When the rice is ready add it in the pan with the zucchini mix and stir it for 3 min in low fire..
  5. Serve it with parmesan if you like.Enjoy!.
