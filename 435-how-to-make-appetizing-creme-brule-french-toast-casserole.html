Creme Brulé French toast casserole instructions

  1. Melt butter, sugar, and syrup in sauce pan over medium heat. Once smooth, pour into 13 x 9 baking dish..
  2. Cut 6 slices of bread into small squares and arrange in one layer in baking dish..
  3. In a mixing bowl, whisk eggs, cream, vanilla, Chambord, and salt..
  4. Pour over bread. Cover. Chill over night..
  5. Preheat to 350°. Bring dish to room temp and bake 40 min..
