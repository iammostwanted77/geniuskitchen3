Ji-Fan Dimsum • Chicken Porcupine • Chicken Flower Dumpling step by step

  1. In a medium bowl, add all thr ingredients except rice and water..
  2. Let the mix marinate for 10 minutes..
  3. Line the Bamboo Basket with Banana Leaf and set aside..
  4. Heat 800 ml water in wok till it bubbles. Keep it covered with lid..
  5. Roll out 8 equal pieces of the chicken mix..
  6. Spread the presoaked rice over a plate and roll the chicken Balls over it firmly and place on the Banana leaf lined in the Bamboo Basket. Keep a little space for allowing expansion of rice..
  7. Push in 1 toothpick stick in each dumpling to help pick up after cooking..
  8. Place the Bamboo Basket in the warm water wok and cover it with lid..
  9. Steam the basket for 20-25 minutes or till the rice is steamed well..
  10. After 20-25 serve the Dumplings immediately with Oriental Sauce/Dip/Momo Sauce or just have it without anything..
  11. Relish the super delicate healthy dish..
