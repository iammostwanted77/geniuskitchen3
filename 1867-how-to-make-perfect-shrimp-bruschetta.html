Shrimp Bruschetta instructions

  1. Boil 4 roma tomatoes.
  2. Slice half cup basil.
  3. Add 3 minced garlic cloves.
  4. Add 1/3 cup olive oil.
  5. Add 1 teaspoon of Italian seasoning.
  6. Ad for cracks of peppers.
  7. Peel and chop the Roma tomatoes.
  8. Add tomatoes to mixture.
  9. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil with crushed red pepper.
  10. Add clean, peel, and devained shrimp to olive oil.
  11. Add 1 teaspoon of Italian seasoning.
  12. Slice and base the bread with the olive oil mixture.
  13. Allow the bread to prebake with the olive oil for about 5 minutes or until crispy.
  14. Add chopped cooked shrimp to the tomato and basil mixture.
  15. Place the mixture on the bread and topped with mixed Italian cheese and bake for another 5 minutes.
