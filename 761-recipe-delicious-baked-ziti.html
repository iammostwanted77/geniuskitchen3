Ingredients of Baked ziti

  1. It's 1 lb of ground beef (or turkey).
  2. You need 1/2 lb of mild italian sausage.
  3. Prepare 1 can (24 oz) of spaghetti sauce.
  4. You need 1/2 of onion (diced).
  5. It's 3 cloves of garlic (minced).
  6. Prepare 16 oz. of mozzerella (shredded).
  7. Prepare 16 oz. of rotelle pasta.
  8. It's of Salt/pepper.
  9. It's of Parmesan cheese.

Baked ziti step by step

  1. Bring pan of water to boil. Add salt and pasta..cook until they have a little bit of chew to them still 8-10 min. Drain and return to pan..
  2. Brown sausage with a little bit of olive oil. Remove from pan..
  3. Add onion to drippings from sausage, saute. Add garlic just before onions are done..
  4. Add ground beef to onions and garlic, brown and then drain fat. Return to heat and add sausage and sauce. Let simmer.
  5. In baking dish coat bottom with sauce, add layer of cheese followed by a layer of pasta.. Repeat until all is used..top with a final layer of cheese and parm..
  6. Bake at 350°F for 20 min. Cheese should be melted and slightly golden brown..
  7. Serve and enjoy!!!.
