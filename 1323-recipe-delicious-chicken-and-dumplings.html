Ingredients of Chicken and dumplings

  1. Prepare 2 of cooked chicken brest.
  2. Prepare 2 can of mixed vegetables.
  3. Prepare 1 can of cream of chicken soup.
  4. Prepare 2 cup of bisquick.
  5. It's 2/3 cup of milk.
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp of sugar.
  7. You need 1 of salt and pepper to taste.

Chicken and dumplings step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Cut chicken in chunks place in 8x12 pan.
  3. Add mixed vegetables do not drain.
  4. Add cream of chicken soup.
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste mix and place in oven.
  6. Bake for 1 hr..
  7. Meanwhile in separate bowl mix bisquick. Milk and sugar.
  8. After chicken has baked spoon dumpling mix on chicken and bake for another 15 minutes or until golden brown.
  9. Enjoy.
