Cilantro -Lime Vinaigrette instructions

  1. Mise en place all ingredients.
  2. Blend shallots, lime juice, garlic, sugar until emulsified.
  3. Gradually add olive oil, allowing oil to infuse with the lime mixture. Then add cilantro blend into for another 15 - 20 sec until cilantro is mixed in.
  4. Add lime zest for additional taste for your desire.
