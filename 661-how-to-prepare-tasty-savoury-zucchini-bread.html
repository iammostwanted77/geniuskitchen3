Ingredients of Savoury Zucchini Bread

  1. Prepare 1 +1/2 cup of whole wheat flour.
  2. It's 1/2 cup of oats flour.
  3. It's 1 +1/4 cup of grated zucchini.
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup of olive oil.
  5. You need 1 tbsp of chia seeds soaked in 1/2 cup water.
  6. It's 1/4 cup of almond milk/ dairy milk.
  7. It's 1/4 tsp of garlic powder.
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp of onion powder.
  9. You need 1 tbsp of baking powder.
  10. Prepare 1 tsp of baking soda.

  13. Prepare 1 tbsp of lemon juice.
  14. You need 1/2 tbsp of lemon zest.

Savoury Zucchini Bread instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl combine whole wheat flour, oats flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, sugar, garlic powder, onion powder, lemon zest, Italian herbs and chilli flakes. Now in another bowl mix rest of the ingredients (except zucchini) and mix well..
  2. Then combine the wet and dry ingredients and mix well with a spatula. Now fold in grated zucchini. Transfer the batter to the lined baking mould and bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean and the crust becomes nice and golden brown. Take it out and let it cool.
