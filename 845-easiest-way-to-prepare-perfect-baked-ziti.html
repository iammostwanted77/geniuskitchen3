Baked Ziti.

You can have Baked Ziti using 7 ingredients and 8 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of Baked Ziti

  1. Prepare 1 Pound of Beef , ground.
  2. Prepare 16 Ounces of Pasta Ziti.
  3. It's 1 (26 oz) Jar of Spaghetti Sauce.
  4. It's 1 Clove of Garlic.
  5. You need 1 of Onion , chopped.
  6. It's 2 Cups of Cheese Mozarella , shredded.
  7. You need of Parmesan Cheese , grated.

Baked Ziti step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 375F..
  2. Cook pasta according to directions until al-dente. Drain..
  3. Combine garlic, onion, and ground beef in a large skillet. Cook over medium heat until ground beef is brown. Drain any excess fat..
  4. Add spaghetti sauce to meat mixture and simmer 3-5 minutes until heated through..
  5. In a 9x13 baking dish or large casserole dish, put a small amount of meat/sauce mixture in the bottom to prevent burning..
  6. Layer as follows: pasta, meat/sauce mixture, 1 cup mozzarella cheese, pasta, meat/sauce, 1 cup mozzarella cheese. Top off with a sprinkle (or more!) of grated parmesan cheese..
  7. Bake for 30-40 minutes until cheese is melted and sauce is bubbly..
  8. Let stand about 5 minutes before serving..
