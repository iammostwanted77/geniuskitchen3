Ingredients of Classic Shepard’s Pie

Classic Shepard’s Pie instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a non stick pan and add onions and garlic.
  2. Sauté for 2-3 minutes on medium heat and add chopped veggies followed by minced meat.
  3. Next add, salt to taste, 1 tbspn crushed black pepper and Worcestershire sauce.
  4. Mix well and sauté for 5-7 minutes on medium heat.
  5. Add paprika and mix well.
  6. Reduce heat. Add meat stock..
  7. Cook covered until veggies soften.
  8. Meanwhile pressure cook or boil the potatoes.
  9. After they cool off, deskin and mash them.
  10. Place them in a bowl and add butter. Mix well.
  11. Add salt to taste, remaining black pepper, milk and cheese.
  12. Mix all the ingredients well.
  13. Once the veggies soften and meat is semi cooked, take the pan off heat.
  14. Grease a baking dish with some oil or melted butter.
  15. Add the meat-veggies mix and layer well with the help of a spatula.
  16. Add the mashed potato on TOP.spread and layer evenly over the minced meat layer, followed by parmesan cheese.
  17. Bake at 180 degree celcius for 30-40 minutes or until the potato layer shows a lovely golden brown colour.
  18. Take off oven and let cool a bit for sometime.
  19. Sprinkle some finely chopped parsley on top(Optional).
  20. Serve hot accompanied with breads or soups of your choice.
  21. Bon Appetit!.
