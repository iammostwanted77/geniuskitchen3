  5. You need 1/2 of vadalia onion.

  8. Prepare 1 tbsp of chicken flavored better that bullion.
  9. It's 1/2 tsp of Italian seasoning.
  10. It's 1/2 tsp of dried parsley or a half a bunch fresh chopped.
  11. You need 2 of peeled chopped russet potatoes.
  12. You need 1 head of garlic peeled(my family loves garlic and it wasn't too strong)adjust to ur taste.
  13. You need 1/2 of rolled out ball cut and dried of my homemade noodles.

Kayla's chicken and dumpling noodles step by step

  1. Premake noodles I have a recipe if u dont.
  2. Add all veggies and garlic and pulse in food processor or chop.
  3. Saute in olive oil for 3 or 4 minutes add chicken broth.
  4. Add chicken breast and cook till done all the way threw and chop.
  5. Add better than bullion and seasonings.
  6. Add in chopped potatoes.
  7. Boil two minutes then add noodles a few at a time not touching one another and stir every couple u add.
  8. Cook about 4 minutes or longer if you like them softer like I do.I cooked mine about 8 minutes.check that your potatoes are fork tender and taste test your noodles.
