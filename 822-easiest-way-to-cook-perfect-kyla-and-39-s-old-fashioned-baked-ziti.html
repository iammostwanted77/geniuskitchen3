Kyla's Old Fashioned Baked Ziti step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F.
  2. Start boiling water for Ziti. Meanwhile, remove skin from sausage to make it like ground beef. & cut your chicken into inch size pieces..
  3. Put uncooked pasta in boiling water, & in medium skillet cook sausage. When fully cooked, remove & set aside. Then cook chicken in sausage oil until cooked thru. When done, mix together chicken & sausage & sprinkle with spices..
  4. Once pasta & Meat are done, drain pasta, set aside. In a 9x13 baking dish, put Ziti in a single layer in bottom of dish, followed by a layer of sauce, then meat mixture, then Italian cheese. Repeat until you've used all ingredients..
  5. Top dish with parmesan cheese. Bake, uncovered just until sauce is boiling & cheese is melted & bubbly..
