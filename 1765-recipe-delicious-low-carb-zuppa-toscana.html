Ingredients of Low Carb Zuppa Toscana

  1. You need 1 lb of Hot Italian Sausage (ground).
  2. You need 6 Slices of Bacon.
  3. Prepare 1 of Medium Onion (chopped).
  4. Prepare 4 Cloves of Garlic (crushed).
  5. You need 1 lb of Radishes (sliced).
  6. You need 1 Bunch of Green Kale (torn into bite-sized pieces).
  7. It's 4 Cans of (or approx. 60 oz) Chicken Broth.
  8. It's 1 Can of Crushed Tomatoes (with liquid).
  9. Prepare 1 Cup of Heavy Whipping Cream.
  10. Prepare 2 Tbsp of Butter.
  11. You need of Salt.
  12. You need of Pepper.
  13. Prepare of Red Pepper Flakes.

Low Carb Zuppa Toscana instructions

  1. Cook bacon, chop and set aside. Reserve grease..
  2. Combine bacon grease and butter a large stockpot. Bring to medium heat and add garlic and onion. Saute until soft (5 min)..
  3. Add sausage, mix well. Stir occasionally until browned..
  4. Add chicken broth, bacon, tomatoes, and radishes. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer until radishes are soft (20 min)..
  5. Remove from heat. Add Kale and Cream, stir until well mixed..
  6. Add salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes to taste..
