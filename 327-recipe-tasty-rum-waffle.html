Ingredients of Rum Waffle

  1. Prepare 3 cups of flour.
  2. It's 2 cups of sugar.
  3. It's 3 teaspoon of rum extract.
  4. You need 3 of eggs.
  5. It's 1 of tespoon baking soda.
  6. You need 1 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder.
  7. You need 2 tablespoon of butter.
  8. You need 1 teaspoon of cream of tatar.
  9. It's 2 cups of water.
  10. It's 1 teaspoon of water.
  11. You need 1/3 cup of oil.

Rum Waffle step by step

  1. In a medium size bowl add the 2 cups water and 2 cups sugar and mix it using a spoon for 5minutes..
  2. In a small bowl add the 3 eggs,2 tablespoon butter, 1/3 cup oil and the1 teaspoon water and mix it using a mixer in medium for 5minutes..
  3. Add sugar mixture and the egg mixture in a large bowl and mix it again using a mixer mix it for 5 minutes..
  4. After it mix add 3 cups flour, 1 teaspoon cream of tartar,1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder,1 teaspoon baking soda, and the 2 tablespoon butter and mix it again with a mixer for 10 minute or at least until it is combined..
  5. After it is mix at 3 teaspoon rum slowly to the mixture and then mix it up for 5 minutes.
  6. Then using a ice cream scoop take 2 scoop of the batter and then you put them into the machine and cook it until golden brown or usually I cook it for 10 minutes..
