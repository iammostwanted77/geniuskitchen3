Ingredients of Mahi Mahi Fritters

  1. Prepare 200 g of mahi mahi.
  2. It's 1 piece of grated garlic.
  3. You need 1 piece of grated ginger.
  4. It's 2 Tbsp of soy sauce.
  5. You need 1 Tbsp of sake.
  6. You need 3 Tbsp of olive oil.
  7. It's 2 Tbsp of starch.
  8. You need of Oil for frying.

Mahi Mahi Fritters instructions

  1. Remove any skin on the mahi mahi and cut into smaller pieces..
  2. Put the garlic, ginger, soy sauce, sake and mahi mahi into a plastic baggie, and mix together well. Leave for at least 30 minutes up to a half day..
  3. Just before frying, put the starch into the bag, and shake contents to mix well..
  4. Heat oil in a sauce pan and fry the mahi mahi until it begins to get crispy. Turn and fry until it is well cooked..
