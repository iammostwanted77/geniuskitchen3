Ingredients of Chicken Momos [ Dumplings ]

  1. It's of Do dough :.
  2. Prepare 120 g of refined flour.
  3. It's 1/4 tsp of baking powder.
  4. It's 1/2 tsp of salt water (for kneading).
  5. Prepare of For chicken stuffing :.
  6. It's 1 cup of chicken [ minced ].
  7. You need 1/2 cups of onion, finely chopped.
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp of black pepper powder.
  9. It's 1 tsp of oil.
  10. You need 1/2 tsp of garlic paste.
  11. Prepare 1/2 tsp of soya sauce.
  12. Prepare of For chilli sauce :.
  13. You need 25 g of garlic, peeled.
  14. Prepare 6 g of whole red chilies.
  15. Prepare 3 tsp of vinegar.
  16. It's To taste of salt.
  17. It's To taste of sugar.

Chicken Momos [ Dumplings ] instructions

  1. Steps to prepare Chilli Sauce: Cut the red chillies and soak them in water for about two hours..
  2. Cut up into small pieces and soak in the vinegar for one to two hours..
  3. Put all the ingredients in a mixie and grind to a smooth paste..
  4. Steps to prepare momos: In a bowl mix all the ingredients of the chicken, Keep aside..
  5. Knead the refined flour, baking powder and salt together into firm dough. Cover and keep aside for 30 minutes..
  6. Roll the dough into very thin 4-5 inch balls..
  7. Take each ball and place some filling in the center..
  8. Bring the edges together and twist to seal it..
  9. Steam in a steamer for about 10 minutes and serve hot with chilly sauce..
