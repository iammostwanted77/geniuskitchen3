30 minute Chicken and Dumplings step by step

  1. Preferably in a Dutch Oven, over medium heat, add chicken broth, water, butter, and all seasonings. Stir to combine, and then bring the soup mixture up to a boil.
  2. Add the skinned, deboned, and shredded rotisserie chicken to the soup mixture. Stir to combine, and cover.
  3. Place the all-purpose flour and milk into a sealable container, and shake vigorously to create a "slurry. " Now SLOWLY add to the soup mixture, while stirring constantly. (Another option; you can add about a 1/4 cup of the soup mixture TO the slurry to bring it up to temperature, called tempering, and then add the whole thing to the soup mixture).
  4. Add the frozen peas and carrots, stir, then cover..
  5. FOR DUMPLINGS.
  6. Combine the all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt. Mix well with a fork..
  7. Now add the butter and milk and stir until combined..
  8. Remove the cover, and drop dumplings into the boiling soup mixture 1 tablespoon at a time. Using a tablespoon scoop makes this step a lot easier, and creates even dumplings!.
  9. Reduce heat to a simmer, and cover. Cook for 10 minutes, DO NOT OPEN THE LID FOR THE WHOLE TEN MINUTES! After the 10 minutes has passed, uncover, serve and enjoy!.
