Spicy Zuppa Toscana step by step

  1. Sauté the onion and garlic in olive oil for 2 min.
  2. Squeeze the sausage out of the casing into the pan.
  3. Sauté 5 min.
  4. Add the broth and potatoes.
  5. Simmer for 15 min, covered.
  6. Combine cream and flour until smooth and add into pan.
  7. Stir and simmer 10 min.
  8. Add kale and simmer for 5 more min.
  9. Add salt and pepper as desired and serve into bowls.
  10. Slurp up all the goodness!.
