Ingredients of Patriotic Sangria

  1. You need 1 of bottle dry white wine.
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup of triple sec.
  3. You need 1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice.
  4. It's 1/4 cup of simple syrup.
  5. It's 1/4 cup of flavored vodka (citrus or berry flavored ).
  6. You need 3/4 cup of blueberries.
  7. It's 3/4 cup of raspberries.
  8. You need 3/4 cup of strawberries, hulled and sliced.
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup of pineapple chunks (optional: buy a star shaped cookie cutter and cut pineapple stars out of fresh whole pineapple slices).
