Ingredients of One pan chicken parmesan pasta skillet

  1. Prepare 3 of - Boneless skinless Chicken Breasts.
  2. You need 2 tablespoons of - Italian dressing.
  3. Prepare To taste of - Salt & Pepper.
  4. You need 16 ounces of - (small/medium sized) Rigatoni pasta.
  5. It's 24 ounces of - Pasta Sauce (marinara works but Alfredo is better).
  6. It's 24 ounces of - water.
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup of - Parmesan cheese.
  8. It's 1/2 cup of - Shredded Mozzarella cheese.

One pan chicken parmesan pasta skillet instructions

  1. Defrost and cube the Chicken Breasts, 1×1in. or 2×2in (your preference).
  2. In a large 12in. skillet, warm Italian Dressing over medium/high heat..
  3. Add the cubed Chicken Breasts, Salt&Pepper to taste. Cook for 5-10min or until edges are browned..
  4. Add Rigatoni Pasta and Pasta Sauce..
  5. Take the, now empty, jar of Pasta Sauce and fill with water. Add the water to the skillet. Mix/Stir well..
  6. Bring to boil for 2min.
  7. Stir, reduce heat to a simmer. COVER, and let it cook for 20-30min. Stir every 5min.
  8. Remove the cover for 10min and let extra moisture evaporate..
  9. Add the Parmesan and the Mozzarella cheese just before surviving. Or, I like too, put it in the oven for 5min on broil. It makes the cheese perfect!.
