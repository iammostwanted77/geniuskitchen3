chicken and dumplings step by step

  2. dice onion, and celery.
  3. combine chicken, vegetables, butter, and stock into a large stew pot.
  4. cook the above until chicken is fully cooked about 20 minutes.
  5. combine dumpling ingredients.
  6. form dumpling dough into golf ball size being careful not to pack the dough.
  7. drop dumplings into boiling chicken soup, cover and cook 15 minutes.
  8. remove from heat and let stand 15 minutes.
