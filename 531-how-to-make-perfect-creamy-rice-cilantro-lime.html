Creamy rice cilantro lime step by step

  1. Cook first three ingredients on stove just like you are cooking rice with package directions, but use the vegetable broth and water..
  2. Above picture is how mine turned out Then allow to cool.
  3. In sauté pan, sweat onions and garlic.
  4. Add to that lime zest and cilantro. Looks like this when done.
  5. Cook just a liitle longer and add all juice from the two limes.
  6. Once the sautéed mixture has cooled a bit, place all in a food processor or blender.
  7. Once in food processor, blend slowly and start adding the milk, or cream if you choose, to the onion and cilantro mixture. Mine came out like this.
  8. Now take the cooled cooked rice, put in a casserole dish for the oven large enough for all ingredients.
  9. The combine all together in a prepared casserole dish. Stir in some shredded cheddar cheese if you like and maybe some pads of butter. I like to add some cheddar cheese to the top and a little Panko for crunch. This is what mine looked like just before the oven.
  10. Cook in center of oven 350 degrees for about 15 - 20 minutes done. Enjoy..
