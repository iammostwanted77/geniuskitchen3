My big sister's chicken and dumplings instructions

  1. Pull chicken off the bones and shred with your fingers.
  2. Pour gravy in a pot..
  3. Add chicken and veggies to the pot..
  4. Let it get hot, stir..
  5. In a bowl, mix bisquick and water to a thick texture, add the water a little at a time..
  6. Drop globs of the mixture onto the gravy concoction..
  7. Cover and let cook on medium low for 15 minutes.
  8. Voila!.
