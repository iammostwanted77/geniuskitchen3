Easy Basic Sweet Potato Pies.

You can cook Easy Basic Sweet Potato Pies using 6 ingredients and 15 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Easy Basic Sweet Potato Pies

  1. It's 2 of Frozen puff pastry.
  2. You need 150 grams of Sweet potato.
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp of Light brown sugar (or sugar).
  4. You need 10 grams of Butter.
  5. You need 2 of to 3 drops Vanilla essence.
  6. It's 1 of Egg yolk.

Easy Basic Sweet Potato Pies instructions

  1. First, make the sweet potato paste. Chop the sweet potatoes into 1 cm cubes, then in a sauce pan, boil in water filled to the level of the sweet potatoes..
  2. Transfer the sweet potatoes to a bowl, add the light brown sugar, butter, and vanilla essence while hot, then thoroughly mix while mashing..
  3. If the paste is too dry, add milk or heavy cream to adjust to desired consistency..
  4. Preheat the oven to 210°C..
  5. Remove the frozen puff pastries from the freezer, thaw for 5 to 10 minutes, then cut each into 4 pieces..
  6. In two of the pieces, make 5 slits in the center of the pastry, as shown..
  7. In the remaining two, prick holes in the pastry with a fork in several places. For pastries with ventilation holes, skip this step..
  8. Repeat Steps 6 to 7 with the other puff pastry. You should have a total of 4 pastries with slits and 4 pierced with holes..
  9. In the center of the pastry pierced with holes, place a generous mound of sweet potato paste, leaving a 7 to 8 mm perimeter around the edges (to seal the pastry)..
  10. Place the pastry with slits on top, then, press down around the edges using a fork to seal the pastry..
  11. Line a baking tray with parchment paper, arrange the pies on top, brush on the egg yolk wash, then bake in oven for 10 minutes at 210°C..
  12. Reduce temperature to 180°C, then bake for 15 more minutes, then they're done!.
