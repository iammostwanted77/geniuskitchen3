Ingredients of Chilli & Smoked Paprika spiced Butternut & Sweet Potato soup

  1. It's 1 of medium size Butternut.
  2. Prepare 1 of large sweet potato.
  3. Prepare 1 of medium size onion.
  4. You need 2 cloves of garlic.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of thyme.
  6. You need 1 tsp of chilli flakes.
  7. It's 2 tsp of smoked Paprika.
  8. Prepare 1 of stock gel of choice... chicken or veg.
  9. It's Spoonful of creme fraiche to finish.

Chilli & Smoked Paprika spiced Butternut & Sweet Potato soup step by step

  1. Chop all ingredients & cook slow over a medium heat till start to go tender.
  2. Add enough water to cover. Bring to the boil & add stock.
  3. Simmer for 20mins till all veg is soft.
  4. Blend & serve.
