Ingredients of Creamy Paprika Butternut Soup

  1. Prepare 2 of Tblsp olive oil.
  2. It's 1/2 cup of diced onions.
  3. You need 3 cloves of garlic minced.
  4. Prepare of few sprigs fresh thyme.
  5. It's 2 of Tblsp Spanish smoked paprika.
  6. Prepare 2 Tsp of cumin powder.
  7. You need 1 Tsp of turmeric.
  8. Prepare 1 kg of diced butternut.
  9. It's 2 Tsp of brown sugar.
  10. You need 2 cups of vegetable stock.
  11. It's 250 ml of fresh cream.
  12. Prepare to taste of salt and pepper.
  13. It's of hand/stick blender or food processor.

Creamy Paprika Butternut Soup instructions

  1. Sauté the onions until soft and light brown.
  2. Add garlic, thyme, spices and cook out for 1 minute on medium heat.
  3. Add butternut, brown sugar and vegetable stock. reduce heat and cover pot with lid.
  4. When the butternut is soft (about 20 to 30 minutes later) take off the stove and blitz with the blender.
  5. Add cream, salt and pepper to taste and bring back up to the boil.
  6. Serve hot with fresh ciabatta and a little extra cream if you like.
