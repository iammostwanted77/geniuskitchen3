Ingredients of Dave's Chicken Noodle Soup

  1. It's 2 bags of Reames frozen egg noodles.
  2. Prepare 16 cups of water.
  3. Prepare 3 lbs of chicken.
  4. Prepare 2 lbs of carrots.
  5. Prepare 1 stalk of celery.
  6. You need 1 of sweet onion.
  7. It's 1 jar of Wyler's chicken powder.
  8. You need to taste of Salt.
  9. Prepare to taste of Pepper.
  10. You need to taste of Parsley.
  11. Prepare to taste of Jane's Crazy Mixed-Up Peppers.
  12. Prepare 2 sticks of butter.
  13. Prepare 1 of large can cream of chicken soup.

Dave's Chicken Noodle Soup instructions

  1. Precooked chicken in oil and beer, shred or cut. Add to stockpot..
  2. Slice/chop veggies, add to stockpot..
  3. Add water, butter and seasonings to stockpot. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer, covered until veggies are soft..
  4. Add noodles and cream of chicken soup. Cook until noodles are done..
