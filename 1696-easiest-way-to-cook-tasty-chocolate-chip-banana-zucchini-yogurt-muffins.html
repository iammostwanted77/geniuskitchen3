Ingredients of Chocolate Chip Banana Zucchini Yogurt Muffins

  1. You need 1 box of banana bread mix.
  2. It's 1 cup of shredded zucchini.
  3. It's 1 cup of yogurt.
  4. Prepare 1 bag of chocolate chips.
  5. Prepare 1 of large or 2 small mashed bananas.
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup of Oil.
  7. It's 1 cup of Water.
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp of Cinnamon.
  9. Prepare 1/4 cup of Brown sugar.

Chocolate Chip Banana Zucchini Yogurt Muffins step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 425Â°. Combine banana bread mix and all other ingredients (*except chocolate chips) in a large mixing bowl and beat on low for 1-2 mins..
  2. Once mixture is thoroughly combined, slowly fold in chocolate chips. Pour into cupcake baking tins or bread loaf baking pans, or both..
  3. Bake in oven for 30-45 mins or until muffins and/or bread are passing the "toothpick test" and are a nice golden color around the edges and tops..
  4. Top with extra sprinkles of brown sugar, extra chocolate chips, frosting, or whatever you desire! Enjoy!.
