Ingredients of Ham waffles

  1. Prepare 2 cups of sifted flour.
  2. It's 3 teaspoons of baking powder.
  3. Prepare 2 teaspoons of sugar.
  4. You need 3 of eggs.
  5. Prepare 11/4 cups of milk.
  6. Prepare 4 tablespoons of melted butter.
  7. You need 1/2 teaspoons of salt.
  8. Prepare 2 tablespoons of ham per waffle.

Ham waffles step by step

  1. Sift flour and measure then sissy the flour salt sugar and baking powder three times.
  2. To the well beaten egg yolks add milk and flour alternately.
  3. Add the butter melted over hot water.
  4. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites.
  5. Spoon into hot waffle iron.
  6. Sprinkle ham over top.
