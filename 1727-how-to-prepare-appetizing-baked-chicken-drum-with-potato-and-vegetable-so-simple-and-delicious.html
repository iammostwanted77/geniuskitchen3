Ingredients of Baked Chicken Drum with Potato and Vegetable | So simple and delicious!!!

  1. You need 600 gr of chicken drum.
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp of mixture of garlic paste & onion paste (can be replaced with powder).
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp of Paprika powder.
  4. You need 1 tbsp of honey.
  5. It's 1 tbsp of olive oil.
  6. Prepare 1/4 of Lemon.
  7. You need of Oregano, parsley, rosemary, thyme, Black pepper, turmeric powder, chili powder, curry powder (I use 1/2 tsp each of them).
  8. It's 1 pinch of nutmeg.
  9. It's to taste of Salt.
  10. Prepare 250 gr of potatoes (medium size, cut into 4).
  11. It's 1/2 of Paprika, 1 spring onion, 1 onion, 1/2 zucchini, 1 carrot (cut all the vegetables into bite size pieces).

Baked Chicken Drum with Potato and Vegetable | So simple and delicious!!! instructions

  1. Preheat your oven into 200 degree Celcius..
  2. Score the top of the chicken in a few places with a sharp knife, so that all spices can be absorbed perfectly..
  3. In a bowl, mix all the spices, lemon, honey, olive oil, and salt. Once your marinade is combined, add the chicken, and make sure its well coated..
  4. Place the chicken in a baking dish..
  5. After all chicken are placed in a baking dish, add vegetable and potatoes in to the bowl, and coat it with the rest of the marinade..
  6. Place the vegetable and potatoes around the chicken in the same baking dish..
  7. Bake for 45 minutes with 200 degree Celcius, or until the chicken are cooked well..
