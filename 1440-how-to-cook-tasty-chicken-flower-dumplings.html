Ingredients of Chicken Flower Dumplings

  1. Prepare 400 of gms. chicken mince.
  2. You need to taste of salt.
  3. Prepare handful of coriander leaves, chopped.
  4. It's 2 tbsp. of fried onions.
  5. You need 1 tsp. of ginger-garlic paste.
  6. Prepare 1 tsp. of red chilli flakes (opt).
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup of rice, soaked in water for an hour & drained.

Chicken Flower Dumplings step by step

  1. In a bowl, mix together all the above mentioned ingredients (except rice). Grease your palms and make small round balls out of the mix..
  2. Coat with the soaked rice..
  3. And steam them for 15-17 minutes or till the rice is fully cooked..
  4. Let them cool down for some time..
  5. Serve with tomato ketchup / momo sauce..
