Simple Butternut Squash Soup instructions

  1. preheat oven to 380°F.
  2. remove skin of squash and cube (discard squash guts).
  3. bake squash till tender (about 45 mins).
  4. bring chicken broth, water, and garlic powder to a boil in a large pot.
  5. add squash cubes to boiling liquids.
  6. puree with immersion blender till smooth.
