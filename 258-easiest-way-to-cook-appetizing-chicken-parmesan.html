Chicken parmesan instructions

  1. Place chicken breasts between 2 pieces of plastic wrap and pound out to 1/2 sickness using a mallet or rolling pin s.
  2. Combine bread crumbs 1 quarter cup Parmesan Oregono basil and cayenne in a pie plate.
  3. coat each breast in the flower shaking of any excess flour.
  4. dip each breast in the egg and then into the bread crumb mixture.
  5. Set coated chicken aside on a plate.
  6. Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet.
  7. Carefully add chicken to pan cooking 2 pieces at a time.
  8. Cook until golden on both sides.
  9. Spread 1 cup of tomato sauce in the tomato sauce in the bottom of a baking dish and top with golden chicken breasts.
  10. Top of each breast with the remaining sauce and sprinkle with mozzarella and parmesan cheeses.
  11. Bake in a 375°F of in for 20 minutes or until cheese is bubbly.
  12. Serve hot with spaghetti and tomato sauce.
