Chicken and Dumplings step by step

  1. Boil chicken breast in water until cooked all the way through. Shred or chop chicken. Chop carrots, celery, and onion..
  2. Dissolve chicken boullion cubes in 5 cups of water in large pot. Add chicken, carrots, celery, frozen peas, and onion to stock. Bring to boil..
  3. Add 2 cans of Cream of Chicken Soup and 1 cup of Milk. Whisk until smooth. (Add flour by the Tablespoon as desired to thicken and season with salt/garlic powder/pepper to taste). Simmer on low for 30 minutes..
  4. Mix Bisquick and Milk until smooth. Spoon in dumplings. Cook 10 minutes uncovered, then cover and cook another 10 minutes..
