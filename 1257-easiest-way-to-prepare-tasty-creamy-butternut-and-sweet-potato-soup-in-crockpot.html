  1. Prepare 2 medium of Butternut squash.
  2. You need 3 medium of Sweet potato.

  4. It's 1 large of Potato.

  6. It's 1 1/2 tsp of Cinnamon.

  8. It's of Enough water to cover the ingredients fully.
