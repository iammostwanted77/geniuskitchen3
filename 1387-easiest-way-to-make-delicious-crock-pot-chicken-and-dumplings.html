Crock Pot Chicken and Dumplings instructions

  1. Place thawed chicken, diced onion, cream of chicken, broth, salt and pepper in crock pot on low for 4 hours..
  2. Add cut carrots and celery per preference to crock pot and continue to cook on low for 2 hours..
  3. Turn crock pot on high and add biscuits torn in fourths. Cook for approximately 30 minutes or until done..
  4. Shred chicken and serve..
