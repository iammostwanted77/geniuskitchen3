  8. You need 1 tbsp of black pepper.

MOMS POOR MANS SHEPARD PIE step by step

  1. CUT POTATOES INTO 4'S-BOIL FOR ABOUT 10 MINS UNTIL TENDER.
  2. BROWN GROUND BEEF W/GARLIC-BASIL & OREGANO.
  3. DRAIN POTATOES - DRAIN 1/2 GROUND BEEF FAT (POTATOES WILL SOAK UP REMAINING).
  4. MIX POTATOES AND BEEF-ADD CORN-SIMMER FOR 10 MINS-ADD SALT & PEPPER.
  5. ADD CHEDDAR CHEESE - ENJOY.
