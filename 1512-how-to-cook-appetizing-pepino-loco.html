Pepino Loco step by step

  1. Cut the skin off the cucumber. Cut the tips. Now cut them in half and scrape the inside out. Place them side to side in a long bowl..
  2. Chop up all the fruit..
  3. Add a spoon full of each fruit in the cucumber..
  4. Add a spoon full of Chaka Chaka and nuts.
  5. Spread the lime on top and add very little salt.
  6. Top it off with chamoy..
