Ingredients of Baked ziti and summer vegggies

  2. Prepare 1 tbsp of olive oil.
  3. It's 2 cup of chopped yellow squash.
  4. You need 1 cup of chopped zucchini.
  5. It's 2 cup of chopped tomatoes.
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup of chopped onion.
  7. It's 2 clove of garlic, minced.
  8. It's 1 cup of shredded mozzerella.

  10. You need 2 tsp of oregano.
  11. Prepare 3/4 tsp of salt.
  12. You need 1 tsp of 1/8 crushed red pepper.
  13. Prepare 1/4 cup of part-skim ricotta cheese.

Baked ziti and summer vegggies step by step

  1. cook pasta and drain.
  2. preheat oven to 400.
  3. heat oil in large skillet and add squash, zucchini, and onion, saute for 5 min. add tomato and garlic, saute 3 min. remove from heat, stir in pasta and 1/2 cup mozzerella, herbs and 1/2 tsp salt and pepper..
  4. combine ricotta and egg.and stir into pasta mixture. spoon into sprayed glass or ceramic dish. sprinke with rest or mozzerella. bake foe 15 min or until bubbly..
