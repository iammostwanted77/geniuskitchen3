Pilgrims Pie (Shepards Pie).

You can have Pilgrims Pie (Shepards Pie) using 11 ingredients and 9 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Pilgrims Pie (Shepards Pie)

  1. You need 4 of Potatoes.
  2. You need 2 medium of carrots.
  3. Prepare 1 stick of of celery.
  4. It's 1 of Onion.
  5. Prepare 2 clove of Garlic.
  6. Prepare 2 lb of Beef.
  7. You need 1 of Gravy mix.
  8. It's 1 of Milk (to taste).
  9. Prepare 1 of Butter.
  10. Prepare 1 of Red wine (optional).
  11. You need 1 of Olive oil.

Pilgrims Pie (Shepards Pie) instructions

  1. Cut potatoes into smallish cubes..
  2. Cut onion, carrots, celery, and garlic as small as possible..
  3. Boil potatoes for 15 to 20 minutes, or untill tender..
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan, add carrots and onion, then celery and garlic after, for about 15 to 20 until they're soft (carrots and onion take longest.).
  5. Add red wine (or drink.).
  6. Add beef, gravy, and milk..
  7. Mash potatoes with milk and butter (as much as needed.).
  8. Put meat/vegetable mixture in a 9 x 13 inch baking dish, spread potatoes on top..
  9. Optional: you can also shred cheese over top of potates and bake until cheese is melted..
