Pineapple Zucchini Bread.

You can cook Pineapple Zucchini Bread using 16 ingredients and 8 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Pineapple Zucchini Bread

  1. You need 1 cup of packed brown sugar.
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup of butter softened.
  3. It's 1 cup of shredded zucchini (1 small).
  4. Prepare 1 can of (8 oz) crushed pineapple in juice, undrained, reserving 1 tablespoon liquid.
  5. It's 2 of eggs, slightly beaten.
  6. You need 2 cup of flour.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of baking soda.
  8. Prepare 1 tsp of cinnamon.
  9. It's 1/4 tsp of salt.
  10. Prepare 1/4 tsp of ground allspice.
  11. It's 1/2 cup of chopped walnuts.
  12. Prepare 1 of Glaze.
  13. It's 1/2 cup of powdered sugar.
  14. You need 1 tbsp of Reserved pineapple liquid.
  15. You need 1 tsp of corn syrup.
  16. It's 1/4 tsp of cinnamon.

Pineapple Zucchini Bread instructions

  1. Heat oven to 350..
  2. Grease and flour bottom only of 9 x 5 inch loaf pan..
  3. In large bowl with electric mixer, beat brown sugar and butter on medium speed until light and fluffy..
  4. With spoon, stir in zucchini, pineapple and eggs..
  5. Stir in flour, baking soda, 1 teaspoon cinnamon, sat and allspice until well blended..
  6. Fold in walnuts. Spread evenly in pan..
  7. Bake 60-70 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean. Cool 10 min; remove from on to cooling rack. Cool 30 min..
  8. In small bowl, mix all glaze ingredients until smooth; spoon over warm loaf. Cool completely, about 1 hr. Store in refrigerator..
