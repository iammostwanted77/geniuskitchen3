Ingredients of Alex Guarnaschelli's Mimosa 75

  1. Prepare 3 oz of Gin (preferably Plymouth).
  2. It's 2 of Grated lemons zest.
  3. You need 1 of lemon wedge for garnish.
  4. It's 2 of Tangerine's grated zest.
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp of Honey.
  6. It's 1 1/2 tbsp of Sugar.
  7. You need 1 of 750 ml Bottle of dry champage or other sparkling wine, chilled.

Alex Guarnaschelli's Mimosa 75 step by step

  1. Combine the gin, lemon juice, tangerine juice, and honey in a cocktail shaker or any container with a fitted lid; cover and shake..
  2. Put the sugar on a small plate and sprinkle the lemon and tangerine zests on top. Rub the rims of 6 champagne glasses with the lemon wedge, then gently roll in the zested sugar..
  3. Divide the gin-citrus mixture among 6 glasses and top with champagne..
