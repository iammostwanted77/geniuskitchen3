Beetroot Sangria instructions

  1. Wash, peel and chop beetroot, add ginger, add 1 cup of clean water and blend on high speed..
  2. Sieve using a clean cheesecloth to get the juice.
  3. Now, add the lemon juice, and orange juice (though in this particular recipe, I didn't have orange juice so I used date syrup and the taste was wow)..
  4. Put in the fridge to chill or add ice cubes.
  5. Garnish with cucumber slices and enjoy your sweet, fresh sangria..
