Sweet Potato Pie instructions

  1. In in large bowl mix in flour, mash the potato and break eggs into the bowl and mix everything together.
  2. Add shredded beef, carrot, cabbage and onion and mix properly.
  3. Pre heat oven 220degree and pour pie batter in into a baking pan and bake in the oven for 25minutes.
  4. Remove from oven and serve..
