Easy Chicken Parmesan w/ Penne instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375..
  2. Place tomatoes on a baking sheet. Drizzle olive oil, salt, and pepper over tomatoes..
  3. Let tomatoes cook for about 20 minutes..
  4. While the tomatoes are baking, season chicken breast with salt, pepper, and garlic salt..
  5. Dip each breast in egg, followed by Panko breadcrumbs, and then parmesan cheese..
  6. Brown on each side for ten minutes..
  7. While chicken is browning, boil penne pasta..
  8. Remove tomatoes from oven. Place in food processor with the seasonings, basil and sugar. Blend..
  9. Place chicken back in baking dish and pour your homemade sauce over the breasts..
  10. Top with parsley and mozzarella cheese. Bake for 15 minutes at 375..
