chicken-tofu dumpling step by step

  1. prepare the steamer.
  2. chop the chicken into small pieces.smash. dont use food p.rocessor because it's to smooth.
  3. cut the mushroom in to small pieces. as size of quarter of pinky finger nail.
  4. slice onion springs into small pieces.
  5. mix all together with spatula..
  6. put the filling inside the dumpling skin..
  7. brush the bottom part of the filled dumpling with oil.
  8. put into the steamer. not to close because it will expand.
  9. steam 10 minutes.
