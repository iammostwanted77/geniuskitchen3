Ingredients of Zucchini bread

  1. It's 3 cup of All purpose flour.
  2. It's 1 tsp of Salt.
  3. Prepare 1 tsp of Baking soda.
  4. It's 1 tsp of Baking powder.
  5. Prepare 3 tsp of Ground cinnamon.
  6. It's 3 of Eggs.
  7. You need 1 cup of Vegetable oil.
  8. Prepare 2 1/4 cup of White sugar.
  9. Prepare 3 tsp of Vanilla extract.
  10. It's 2 cup of Grated Zucchini.
  11. Prepare 1 cup of Chopped walnuts.

Zucchini bread step by step

  1. Grease and flour two 8 by 4 inch pans and preheat oven to 325°F.
  2. Sift flour, salt, baking powder, baking soda, and cinnamon in a bowl..
  3. Beat eggs, oil, vanilla, and sugar together in a large bowl..
  4. Add sifted ingredients to creamed mixture and beat. It may be easier to use your hands and make doughy mixture..
  5. Stir in undrained zucchini and nuts until well combined..
  6. Pour in batter in prepared pans. Cook for 40 to 60 minutes until center comes out clean..
  7. Once out of the oven let cool for at least 20 minutes before removing from pan..
