Cilantro cheese stuffed chicken nuggets step by step

  1. Mix chicken and seasoning cilantro, green onion and parsley and veggies in food processor process till it is all ground together.
  2. Cube cheese.
  3. Form chicken into ball.
  4. Add 1 cube of cheese in the center of the chicken wrap chicken around it and form into nuggets.
  5. Take the chicken nuggets and coat with flour, dip in egg and cover with the Bread crumbs.
  6. Fry in oil till golden brown drain on paper towels lined plate or rack.
  7. Serve warm with a side dish l made potato wedges onion rings and pepper rings go perfect and favorite sauces for dipping.
