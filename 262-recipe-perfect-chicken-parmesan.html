chicken parmesan instructions

  1. preheat broiler.
  2. set up three dishes on counter top one with flower one with eggs and the other with a mixture of breadcrumbs half grated cheese herbs and salt and pepper.
  3. place skillet over medium high heat with Bout 3 tablespoons evoo while pan is heating up season chicken with salt pepper and coat each one with flower then egg then breadcrumbs.
  4. while chicken is cooking place a med. sizes pot on stove over med. heat add 2 tablespoons evoo add garlic crushed pepper and chopped onion cook 5-6 min stirring often until veg is soft add tomatoes and heat them through about 2 min remove pot from heat and stir in basil then season sauce with salt and pepper.
  5. layer the chicken with sauce in casserole dish top with remaining grated parmigiano and mozzarella cheeses brown the casserole under broiler for about 3 mins or unfilled cheese is melted and is lightly golden brown add spaghetti and garlic bread if desired.

Cover and cook until cheese is melted. Serve with a side of spaghetti.
