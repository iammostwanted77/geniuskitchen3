Apple-Butternut Soup step by step

  1. coat a dutch over with cooking spray.
  2. add margarine.
  3. place over medium heat until margarine melts. add onion and garlic, saute 5mins or until tender.
  4. add flour, stirring until smooth.
  5. stir in chicken broth and next 4 ingredients. bring to a boil. cover, reduce heat and simmer 45 mins or until squash is tender.
  6. let mixture cool slightly.
  7. add squash mixture to food processor.
  8. return mixture to pan, add milk. cook over medium heat until heated, stirring occasionally. do not boil..
