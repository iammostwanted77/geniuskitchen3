Chenango Zucchini Bread step by step

  1. Grate zucchini, carefully squeeze out much of the liquid, and set aside..
  2. Beat eggs and then add the next 4 ingredients..
  3. Measure 3 cups of flour and add to zucchini mix one cup at a time. Then with the last cup of flour add the rest of the dry ingredients..
  4. Mix well..
  5. Pour into 2 greased loaf pans..
  6. Bake at 350 degrees F for 50 - 60 minutes. Check doneness with a toothpick in the center of the loaves..
