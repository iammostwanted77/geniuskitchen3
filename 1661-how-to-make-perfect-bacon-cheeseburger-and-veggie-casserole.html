Ingredients of Bacon Cheeseburger and Veggie casserole

  1. You need 2-3 lbs of or ground beef.
  2. It's 1/2-1 cups of fresh bacon bits.
  3. Prepare 1-2 of Fresh chopped Bell peppers/bag of frozen bell pepper slices.
  4. It's 1 of small bag of mixed frozen broccoli and cauliflower.
  5. Prepare 4 of eggs.
  6. Prepare 1 cup of heavy cream.
  7. It's 1-2 tablespoons of worchestire sauce.
  8. You need 1 teaspoon of your favorite hot sauce (optional).
  9. It's 1-2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese.
  10. You need 5-10 strips of bacon.

Bacon Cheeseburger and Veggie casserole instructions

  1. Brown ground beef in a pan, chopping it up as it cooks. Drain grease..
  2. Scoop ground beef into a non-stick baking pan. Mix in broccoli, cauliflower, bell peppers, and bacon bits.
  3. Spread shredded cheese along the top layer.
  4. Mix 4 eggs and heavy cream. Whisk until eggs are fully mixed in. Add worchestire sauce, and hot sauce. Mix well..
  5. Pour egg mixture over the entire surface of casserole..
  6. Place in preheated oven at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
  7. Fry bacon strips.
  8. At about 5 minutes left, lay the bacon strips on top of the casserole and place back in the oven for the remaining 5 minutes..
  9. Remove from oven and let set for 5 minutes..
  10. Serve and enjoy.
