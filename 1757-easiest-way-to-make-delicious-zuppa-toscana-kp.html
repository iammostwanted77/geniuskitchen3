Ingredients of Zuppa Toscana KP

  1. Prepare 1 lb of Bulk Italian Sausage.
  2. You need 1/2 tbsp of Red Pepper Flakes.
  3. You need 8 slice of Bacon (cut into 1/2" pieces).
  4. Prepare 1 1/4 large of Onion (diced).
  5. You need 1 1/2 tbsp of Garlic (minced).
  6. You need 6 3/4 can of Chicken Broth (13.75 oz cans).
  7. Prepare 8 medium of Potatoes (sliced 1/4" thick).
  8. It's 2 2/3 cup of Kale (rinsed and chopped).
  9. You need 1 1/3 cup of Heavy Cream.

Zuppa Toscana KP instructions

  1. In a large pot or dutch oven brown Sausage w/ Red Pepper Flakes. Drain, Rinse, Set aside..
  2. In same pot cook Bacon until just crisp. Drain grease except for 3 Tbsp. Add Onions and Garlic. Cook until onions are translucent..
  3. Add in Chicken Broth and bring to a boil. Add in Potatoes and simmer until potatoes are fork tender.
  4. Add Sausage, Kale, and Heavy Cream. Simmer 4-5 minutes..
  5. Serve topped with grated parmesean, and a Breadstick on the side..
