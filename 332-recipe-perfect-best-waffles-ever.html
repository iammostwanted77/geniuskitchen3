Ingredients of Best Waffles EVER

  1. You need 1 Cup of All Purpose Flour.
  2. Prepare 1/4 Cup of Fage Non Fat Greek Yogurt.
  3. You need 1 1/2 Tablespoons of Dark Brown Sugar.
  4. It's 2 Teaspoons of Baking Powder.
  5. Prepare 1/2 Teaspoon of Salt.
  6. You need 5 of Egg Whites from large egg.
  7. You need 1 of Whole large egg.
  8. It's 1/4 Cup of Almond Milk.
  9. You need 1/8 Teaspoon of White Vinegar.
  10. It's 3 Teaspoons of Vanilla Extract.

Best Waffles EVER step by step

  1. Preheat Waffle Iron.
  2. Combine all dry ingredients in a bowl and whisk together.
  3. Combine all wet ingredients in a bowl and whisk vigorously.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients with a spatula. Mix well.
  5. Pour the batter in the waffle iron being careful not to use too much batter, it expands quite a bit during cooking.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy.
