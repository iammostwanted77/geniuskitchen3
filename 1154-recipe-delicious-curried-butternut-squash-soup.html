Ingredients of curried butternut squash soup

  1. Prepare 4 tbsp of butter.
  2. You need 1 medium of onion, finely chopped.
  3. You need 2 of celery ribs, finely chopped.
  4. It's 5 cup of butternut squash, (approx 2 lb).
  5. Prepare 1 cup of carrots, peeled and chopped.
  6. You need 1 1/2 tsp of basil.
  7. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp of curry powder.
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup of dry white wine.
  9. It's 2 1/2 cup of chicken broth.
  10. You need 1 cup of heavy cream.
  11. It's 1 of salt and pepper to taste.

curried butternut squash soup step by step

  1. melt butter in a saucepan. add onion, celery, carrots, basil, and curry.
  2. cook until soft.
  3. add precooked squash, chicken broth, and white wine.
  4. puree in food processor until smooth.
  5. return to saucepan, add cream, salt, and pepper.
  6. garnish with croutons, toasted almonds, or chopped fresh parsley.
  7. precooking squash by cutting in half and placing meat-side down on cookie sheet with water surrounding it, will drastically cut labor by not having to peel and cut squash..
