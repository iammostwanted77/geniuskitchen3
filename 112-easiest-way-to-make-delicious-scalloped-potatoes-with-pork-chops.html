scalloped potatoes with pork chops instructions

  1. preheat oven to 400.
  2. peel potatoes and thinly slice.
  3. in a roasting pan layer potatoes.
  4. add a layer of soup.
  5. layer cheese.
  6. layer pork chops.
  7. repeat steps until pan is full or everything is used.
  8. pour milk over the top.
  9. cook until potatoes and meat are done.
