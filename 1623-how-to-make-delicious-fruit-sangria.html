Ingredients of Fruit sangria

  1. It's 2 cup of orange juice.
  2. You need 1 cup of lemon soda.
  3. You need 2 of chopped strawberry.
  4. It's 1/2 of chopped kiwi.
  5. You need 1/2 of chopped apple.
  6. Prepare 10-12 of mint leaves.
  7. You need 4 tbsp of pineapple puree.
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp of honey.
  9. It's 1 of cinnamon stick.

Fruit sangria step by step

  1. Put all the fruits in a jar and mix gently.
  2. Pour honey,pineapple puree and remaining ingredients except lemon soda and mix well.
  3. Now chill the juice in refrigerator for 2 hour.
  4. Now pour juice in a glass and put lemon soda and serve.
