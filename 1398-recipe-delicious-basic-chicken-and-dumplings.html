Basic Chicken and dumplings instructions

  1. Add water to large stock pot. And bring to a low simmer..
  2. Remove organs and neck from chicken and place in pot..
  3. Cut onion into quarters and discard skin. Add onion to chicken in stock pot. Add at least 1 table spoon sea salt, you can add more later to taste, add fresh cracked pepper. Place lid on pot and continue to simmer covered for 1 1/2 to 2 hours until done..
  4. Remove chicken, once done, and debone. Remove onion and discard.(you can cool chicken stock and remove fat that hardens on top, or skip this step).
  5. Bring stock to a slow boil add two small cans or 1 large can cream of chicken soup. Add milk and return seperated chicken to pot. Watch soup carefully it can easily boil over..
  6. Take a flowered cutting board and roll out biscuits and cut into 1/4" by 2" strips. Taste soup. Add any salt at this time. Add strips a little at a time, stirring after adding them. Continue cooking for 15-30 minutes or until dumplings are to your liking..
  7. Remove from heat soup will be very hot for a long time. You can add vegetables if you want when you add chicken pieces back to soup, carrots, garlic, cellery, ect..
