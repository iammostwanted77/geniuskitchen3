cranberry orange sangria step by step

  1. Rinse cranberries and put in a medium sauce pan. Add just enough water to cover them..
  2. Bring cranberries to a boil add sugar to pan. Bowl until most of the cranberries burst. They will made a pretty loud poping sound. Set aside to cool..
  3. In a large pitcher or dispenser add all ingredients together and stir well. You can drink this right away but I try to let it sit atleast 1 day or longer to really blend the flavors. Keep refrigerated..
