Ingredients of Butternut spinach soup

  1. It's 50 g of butternut.
  2. Prepare 1 of onions.
  3. Prepare 1 clove of garlic.
  4. It's 1 of carrot.
  5. It's 1 of spinach.
  6. You need 1 tbs of cooking oil.
  7. Prepare Pinch of salt.

Butternut spinach soup instructions

  1. Cut the butternut, grate garlic and carrots. In a pan put cooking oil add onions add garlic let it cook..
  2. Add carrot and butternut. Stir and put into a boil add salt..
  3. Add spinach and let them cook.
  4. Blend and enjoy.
