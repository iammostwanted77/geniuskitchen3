  10. It's 1 of kosher salt.

  12. Prepare 1/2 cup of freshly grated parmesan, divided.
  13. You need 1/4 cup of grated pecorino romano.
  14. Prepare 1 lb of fresh mozzarella, grated.

Baked ziti step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Cook pasta until tender but firm, about 6 minutes.
  3. Cook burger in a medium sause pan.
  4. Add onion and mushroom when burger is about halfway done.
  5. Add garlic, cook for about 1 minute.
  6. Add tomatoes, thyme and basil.
  7. Bring to a boil.
  8. Lower heat. simmer covered for 10 min.
  9. Discard sprigs.
  10. Stir in salt and pepper to taste.
  11. Combine pasta and marinara sauce in a big bowl.
  12. Add 1/4 cup of parmesan, pecorino, half the mozzarella.
  13. Season with black pepper and red pepper.
  14. Mix well.
  15. Transfer the pasta to an oiled 9x13 inch baking dish.
  16. Top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan.
  17. Bake until lightly brown, about 25 minutes.
