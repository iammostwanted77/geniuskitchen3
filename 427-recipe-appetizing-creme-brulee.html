Creme Brulee instructions

  1. Add heavy cream to pot and stir on high heat until it becomes frothy (before a boil begins)..
  2. Take from heat and add vanilla..
  3. Mix together in a separate bowl eggs and sugar..
  4. Add cream mix 1 cup at a time to the eggs and sugar mixture..
  5. In a cake pan, put 6 creme brulee cups in and carefully add HOT water to the cake pan. (Should hit 3/4 the way up on the creme brulee cups).
  6. Fill creme brulee cups 3/4 the way with mixture..
  7. Bake 325 for 45 min..
  8. Add 1 TBSP sugar to the top. Broil to harden it or you can torch it to make the sugar hard..
  9. Refrigerate more than 2 hours with plastic covering the tops..
