Sweet potato pie step by step

  1. In a bowlmix together all your dry ingredients.
  2. Add in butter rub in.
  3. Add in egg and water and mix until you get a smooth dough.
  4. Cover allow to rest for 15 mins.
  5. To make the filling boil your potatoe cubes in water until tender.
  6. Heat a skillet,add tablespoon of oil.
  7. Add in the chopped vegetables and minced garlic and stir fry.
  8. Season with maggi and salt.
  9. Add in tbsp of water to thin the filling a bit.
  10. Turn of heat and allow to cool.
  11. Roll out your dough,cut into circles.Spoon filling onto each circle.Brush water on the edges.Flip one side over the other and seal with a fork.
  12. Heat oil and fry until crispy.
