Scalloped Potatoes.

You can cook Scalloped Potatoes using 8 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Scalloped Potatoes

  1. Prepare 2 of Ham Steaks.
  2. Prepare 2 of Large Potatoes.
  3. You need 1/4 Block of Valvita cheese.
  4. Prepare 1 stick of butter.
  5. It's 1 cup of parmigian cheese.
  6. Prepare 2 cups of Milk.
  7. Prepare 2 Tbls of pepper.
  8. Prepare 1 of Small or 1/2 large onion.

Scalloped Potatoes instructions

  1. Preheat oven 350..
  2. Dice both ham steaks, dice onion..
  3. Peal and slice both potatoes.
  4. Grease cake pan and add ham, potatoes, onions.
  5. In a small saucepan melt butter and valvita together over medium heat. Add milk and parmigian cheese, mix together. Add pepper and mix. Pour over the top of potatoes, ham, onion..
  6. Bake at 350 for at least an hour and a half or until potatoes are fully cooked and soft..
