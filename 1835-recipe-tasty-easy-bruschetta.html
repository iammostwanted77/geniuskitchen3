Easy bruschetta instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Slice French bread in into 2 inch pieces place bread pieces on a sheet pan or anything that you would use for oven cooking..
  3. In a bowl mix together all your ingredients. Tomatoes, minced garlic, olive oil, chopped basil, salt, pepper..
  4. Using a spoon, spoon on your mixed ingredients onto pieces of French bread..
  5. Baked in oven at 350 for 10 minutes..
