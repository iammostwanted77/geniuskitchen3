Texas Caviar step by step

  1. Rinse all canned veggies until perfectly clean. Drain.
  2. Dice all fresh veggies so they are the same size as the beans. Toss..
  3. Add salt and the juice of one lime, taste and add the other lime if it isn't tart enough for you..
  4. Toss in the cilantro. Refrigerate. Serve with tortilla chips or as a topping for grilled fish..
