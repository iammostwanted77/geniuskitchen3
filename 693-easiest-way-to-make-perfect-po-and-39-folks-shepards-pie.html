Po'Folks Shepards Pie.

You can have Po'Folks Shepards Pie using 12 ingredients and 9 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Po'Folks Shepards Pie

  1. It's 1 1/2 lb of ground beef.
  2. It's 1/2 medium of onion.
  3. Prepare 4 each of mushroom.
  4. It's 1/2 dash of salt.
  5. You need 1/2 dash of pepper.
  6. You need 1/2 dash of garlic salt.
  7. It's 1/2 dash of preferred seasoning.
  8. You need 4 large of potatoes.
  9. It's 1 packages of cheddar cheese.
  10. You need 1/2 tsp of olive oil.
  11. Prepare 1 can of green beans.
  12. You need 1 can of carrots(optional).

Po'Folks Shepards Pie instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325.
  2. Brown ground beef and drain fat then set aside..
  3. Boil diced potatoes until smooth and season to taste.
  4. Sauteed onion and mushroom until onion is translucent and mushroom is tender.
  5. Heat green beans and season to taste then drain.
  6. Mix onions, mushrooms, ground beef, and greens beans.
  7. Pour ground beef mixture in casserole dish and smooth potatoes on top then add cheddar cheese evenly on top.
  8. Bake for at least 20-35min or until cheese is melted on top.
  9. Enjoy!.
