Ingredients of Butter garlic and chilli Spaghetti

  1. You need of Flat spaghetti.
  2. Prepare 2 tsp of Garlic (finally chopped).
  3. It's of Parmesan cheese.
  4. It's 1 tsp of Chilli flakes.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of Oregano.
  6. It's of Butter.
  7. You need of Salt (as per taste).
  8. It's of Olive oil.

Butter garlic and chilli Spaghetti step by step

  1. Boil water in saucepan. Add salt and oil to water. Let it boil..
  2. Add spaghetti to boiling water and boil to al dente..
  3. Drain and add cold water to stop it further cooking..
  4. In a pan add butter + oil (oil will prevent butter from burning). Add garlic cook for sometime. Add salt and chilli flakes..
  5. Add spaghetti and toss. Cook for 2 min add oregano. Spaghetti is done serve hot and garnish with olive oil and parmesan cheese..
