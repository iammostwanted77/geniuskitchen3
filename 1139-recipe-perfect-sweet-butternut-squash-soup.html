Ingredients of Sweet butternut squash soup

  1. You need of Soup.
  2. Prepare 3 tbsp of butter.
  3. Prepare 1 medium of onion, chopped.
  4. Prepare 2 medium of sweet apples, peeled and chopped.
  5. It's 1 medium of carrot, chopped.
  6. It's 1 medium of butternut squash, peeled and chopped.
  7. Prepare 1 of celery stock, chopped.
  8. It's 48 oz of chicken broth.
  9. Prepare 2 medium of potatoes, peeled and cubed.
  10. It's 1 of salt and pepper to taste.

Sweet butternut squash soup step by step

  1. Put butter and onion in large soup pot on medium heat for about 2 minutes to soften..
  2. Add apples, carrot, celery, squash and potatoes into pot for about 5 minutes or until lightly browned..
  3. Add chicken broth. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover and let simmer for 40 minutes..
  4. With a hand held stick blender, blend soup ingredients until smooth. Serve hot..
