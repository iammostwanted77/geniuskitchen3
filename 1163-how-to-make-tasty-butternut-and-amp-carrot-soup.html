butternut & carrot soup instructions

  1. chop all the vegetables & garlic, place in a saucepan. make up a litre of stock with stock cube & add to vegetables.
  2. add 1 teaspoon of mixed herbs & 1/2 teaspoon parsley into saucepan..
  3. season with salt & pepper to your taste..
  4. bring saucepan to the boil, then reduce heat. simmer until vegetables are cooked..
  5. blend all the ingredients until smooth. enjoy!.
