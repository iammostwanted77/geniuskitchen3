Ingredients of Chicken bacon cheddar ranch egg rolls

  1. You need 1 of boneless skinless chicken breast.
  2. Prepare 3 strips of bacon.
  3. You need 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese.
  4. Prepare of To taste: onion powder, garlic powder, pepper and salt.
  5. You need 10 of egg roll wrappers.
  6. You need of Ranch dressing.

Chicken bacon cheddar ranch egg rolls instructions

  1. Heat fryer or 2-3” of oil to 375. Place chicken in a hot pan and season with spices to taste. Cook until juices run clear and it’s no longer pink. Cut into small cubes..
  2. Mix in cheese. Scoop mixture into one end of all ten wrappers and place a half of a strip of bacon in each one. Roll tightly and fry 5-6 minutes. Serve with ranch to dip!.
