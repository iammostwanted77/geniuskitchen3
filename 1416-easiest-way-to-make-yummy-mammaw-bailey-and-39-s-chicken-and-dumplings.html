Mammaw Bailey's Chicken and Dumplings instructions

  2. Add 2-3 cups broth and mix..
  3. Roll out dough flour and surface..
  4. Then cut into 6 strings and pinch off and drop into boiling broth..
  5. Add some water if needed to make broth less heavey..
  6. Boil about 15 min. or low add chicken..
