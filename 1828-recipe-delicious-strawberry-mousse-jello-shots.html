Strawberry Mousse Jello shots step by step

  1. Grind the strawberries and make a pulp and keep aside.
  2. Melt the white chocolate in microwave.
  3. Add in the cottage cheese and whisk well.
  4. Add milkmaid/condense milk, Taste and add accordingly. I have used less than 1/4 tin..
  5. Add the strawberry pulp and fold in.
  6. FOR SERVING _dip the rim of serving glasses in melted chocolate and dip them in the sprinklers. Refrigerate. You can do this before hand as well..
  7. Pour the white Mousse in the glasses and refrigerate.
  8. Make the jelly according to the packet instructions and let it cool.
  9. Remove our mouse from the refrigerator and pour the jelly.
  10. Refrigerate to set.
  11. Garnish with gems.
