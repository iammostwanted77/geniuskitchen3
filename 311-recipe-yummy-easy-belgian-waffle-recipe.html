Ingredients of Easy Belgian Waffle Recipe

  1. Prepare 2 cups of flour.
  2. You need 2 tbsp of sugar.
  3. It's 1/2 tbsp of salt.
  4. Prepare 2 tsp of baking powder.
  5. It's 1/2 tbs of baking soda.
  6. It's 1 tbsp of vanilla.
  7. Prepare 4 of egg yolk.
  8. It's 1 cup of half & half or milk or buttermilk.
  9. It's 1 stick of melted butter.
  10. Prepare 4 of egg whites.

Easy Belgian Waffle Recipe instructions

  1. First pass all dry ingredients through a strainer in a mixing bowl. Flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, and baking soda. Whisk all dry ingredients together until one..
  2. Separate 4 eggs whites from yolk. Set aside egg whites for later use. In another mixing bowl add melted butter,half and half, vanilla, and butter. Mix well.
  3. Add wet to dry and blend well until batter has a thick consistency..
  4. Lastly, whip your egg whites until it reaches a peak almost making it thick and whip cream like then add your egg whites to the batter. This is what will make your waffles fluffy..
  5. Spray your waffle marker with Pam stray and add a cup of batter..
