Ingredients of 5 cheese baked ziti

5 cheese baked ziti instructions

  1. preheat oven to 350.
  2. cook pasta to package direction (usually 10 minutes at boil stirring occasionally).
  3. brown hamburger meat with garlic, Italian seasoning, and fennel (add spices to taste).
  4. in a bowl mix pasta sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella.
  5. drain pasta and add to sauce mixture.
  6. drain grease from hamburger then gently add to pasta and sauce.
  7. pour and level in a 9x13 glass baking dish.
  8. layer Munster and Colby jack on top, then cover entire dish in sharp cheddar.
  9. bake 30 minutes.
