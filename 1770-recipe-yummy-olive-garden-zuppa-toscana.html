Ingredients of Olive Garden Zuppa toscana

  1. You need 1 lb of Mild Italian Sausage.
  2. You need 2 large of Russet baking potatoes, sliced in half and then in 1/4 inch slices.
  3. You need 1 large of onion chopped.
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup of bacon bit (optional).
  5. It's 2 of garlic cloves, minced.
  6. You need 2 cup of kale or swiss chard, chopped.
  7. It's 32 oz of chicken broth.
  8. You need 1 cup of water.
  9. You need 1 cup of heavy whipping cream.

Olive Garden Zuppa toscana step by step

  1. Brown sausage in your soup pot..
  2. Add chicken broth and water to pot and stir..
  3. Place onions, potatoes and garlic in pot.
  4. Cook on medium heat until potatoes are done..
  5. Add sausage and bacon..
  6. Salt and pepper to taste..
  7. Simmer for another 10 minutes..
  8. Turn to low heat..
  9. Add kale and cream..
  10. Heat through and serve..
