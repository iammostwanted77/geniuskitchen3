Turkey Shepard's pie step by step

  1. cook ground turkey until 80% done and add mushrooms. cook until mushrooms are soft and done. add Italian seasoning and garlic salt to taste. place in a deep baking dish.
  2. cut but not peel red potatoes and boil in pot until soft and ready to be mashed.
  3. mash potatoes and add milk, butter, (Italian seasoning, garlic salt to taste) and then add drained canned corn (mashed potatoes should be firm and thick, not runny or thin).
  4. spread mashed potatoes over ground turkey.
  5. then add cheese over top and bake at 350Â° until cheese is golden brown.
