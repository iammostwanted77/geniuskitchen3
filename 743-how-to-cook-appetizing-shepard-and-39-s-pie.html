Shepard's pie instructions

  1. Peel, wash and boil potatoes, cook minced meat with spices and seasonings till tender, add carrots and peas.
  2. Mash potatoes with some butter till smooth and lump free.
  3. In a baking pan, spread the minced meat and top with a thick layer of the potatoes, beat one egg and brush to top for perfect golden brown..
