Ingredients of Simple Cheesy Italian Baked Ziti

  1. It's 1 box of Garden Rotini Noodles (or whatever noodles you like).
  2. It's 1 lb of Ground Beef.
  3. Prepare 1 lb of Ground Chuck.
  4. You need 1 clove of Garlic Clove chopped finely.
  5. Prepare 1 large of onion, chopped.
  6. It's 1 large of Bell Pepper, chopped.
  7. You need 1 tsp of Oregano.
  8. Prepare 1 tsp of Sweet Basil.
  9. It's 1 tsp of Thyme.
  10. It's of Lawry's Season All Salt.
  11. You need of Lawry's Garlic Salt.
  12. Prepare 1 tbsp of ground black pepper.
  13. It's 1 tsp of cayenne pepper.
  14. It's 2 cup of Shredded parmesan cheese.
  15. Prepare 2 cup of Shredded Cheddar Cheese.
  16. It's 4 cup of Shredded Mozzarella Cheese.
  17. You need 2 can of Chopped tomatoes.
  18. Prepare 2 cup of Basil and onion Spaghetti sauce.
  19. It's 1 can of Tomato Paste.
  20. Prepare 2 cup of Italian cheese blend.

Simple Cheesy Italian Baked Ziti step by step

  1. Pre-heat oven to bake at 375째.
  2. Spray cooking pan with non stick spray and set to the side..
  3. Bring water to boil for noodles. Once water is hot, add noodles and cook until tender..
  4. Mix all spices, onion, bell pepper, garlic, ground beef and ground Chuck together. Mix thoroughly before Browning meat..
  5. Brown meat. Once meat is Brown set to side..
  6. Mix Italian blend cheese, cheddar cheese and parmesan cheese with the meat in a large bowl..
  7. Once cheese has been added, then add the tomatoes, spaghetti sauce and tomato paste to the meat. Stir together thoroughly..
  8. Add meat mix to baking pan in its entirety. Add noodles and then mix the meat with the noodles until all ingredients are blended together..
  9. Add mozzarella cheese to the top of the bake. (I add some extra sprinkles of oregano as well).
  10. Bake at 375째 for 45 minutes to an hour, depending upon how Brown you prefer your cheese..
