  2. You need 1/4 of of red bell pepper -- diced.
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup of black beans - canned -- rinsed and drained.

  7. Prepare 1 ounce of lime juice- fresh squeezed.

  10. It's 2 teaspoons of sugar.
