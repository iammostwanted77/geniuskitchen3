Sweet Potato Pie step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Bake sweet potato(es) until completely tender in foil. Can take up to an hour..
  3. Change oven to 400°F.
  4. Mix sweet potatoes, butter, egg yolks, brown sugar, salt, ginger, cinnamon, nutmeg and evaporated milk. Mix well..
  5. Beat egg whites until stiff peaks form, mix in 1/4 cup of sugar. Add to sweet potato mixture..
  6. Put mixture into pie crust and bake at 400°F for 10 minutes and then reduce heat to 350°F until firm and toothpick or knife comes out clean..
