Recipe: Yummy Baked Ziti

Delicious, fresh and tasty.

Baked Ziti.

You can cook Baked Ziti using 14 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Baked Ziti

  1. Prepare 1 lb of Hot or sweet Italian turkey sausage.
  2. Prepare 1 of onion, chopped.
  3. You need 3 clove of sliced garlic.
  4. It's 28 oz of plain diced tomatoes.
  5. It's 15 oz of tomato sauce.
  6. It's 2 tsp of sugar.
  7. You need 1/4 tsp of salt.
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp of pepper.
  9. Prepare 14 1/2 oz of basil and oregano diced tomatoes.
  10. It's 1 tsp of dried basil leaves or half cup fresh basil chopped.
  11. It's 16 oz of Ziti.
  12. You need 8 oz of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  13. Prepare 1 packages of sliced provolone.
  14. It's 1/4 cup of grated parmesan or romano cheese.

Baked Ziti step by step

  1. Preheat the oven to 375. Crumble the sausage into a 3 quart saucepan. Cook for 8 minutes or until no pink remains. Drain the fat..
  2. In another pot, add the onion and saute for about 4 minutes until soft. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute. Stir in the plain tomatoes with their juice. Stir in the sauce, sugar, salt, and pepper. Simmer, stirring occasionally for 20 minutes. Remove the pan from the heat, and stir in the sausage, seasoned tomatoes, and the basil..
  3. Boil the Ziti, drain, then return to the pot. Coat the Ziti in just enough sauce to coat it. Layer half of the Ziti in a 13 by 9 pan. Top with the provolone overlapping the slices as needed. Add the rest of the Ziti, then the mozzarella cheese. Pour the last of the sauce over the top and sprinkle with parmesan and romano cheese. Bake at 375 for 20-25 minutes until bubbly around the edges and lightly brown. Let it cool for 10 minutes and enjoy!.
  4. Optional: save some of the mozzarella as a topping and add extra seasonings on top before placing it in the oven..
