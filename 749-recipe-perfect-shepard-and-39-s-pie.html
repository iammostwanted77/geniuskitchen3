Shepard's pie step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 200c.
  2. Put potatoes in a sufuria with salted water and boil for 15 minutes or till tender but firm..
  3. In the meantime start on filling..
  4. Pour oil in hot pan then add mince meat,stir for a few minutes till it browns & broken into small pieces..
  5. Add onion, carrots,garlic, Worcestershire sauce, tomato puree, red wine, thyme, rosemary stir and let it cook for 4 minutes.
  6. Add broth and cook for 3 more minutes. Add peas and corn stir a bit and remove from stove.
  7. For the cooked potatoes: drain the water add butter, a bit of milk, salt and pepper to taste then mash. You can add a bit of milk if the consistency is too thick. Milk should be hot..
  8. If it looks okay(not too soft or hard) add the egg yolks and mash.
  9. Scoop the cooked meat mix and place in a large oven safe dish..
  10. Spread the mash on top.
  11. Use the bottom of a spoon to do so.
  12. Sprinkle parmesan cheese on top. If unable to find in shops you can use VERY sharp cheddar..
  13. Poke the above in several places then place in preheated oven for 20 minutes..
