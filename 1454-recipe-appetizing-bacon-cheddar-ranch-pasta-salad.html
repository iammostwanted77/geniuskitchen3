Ingredients of Bacon Cheddar Ranch Pasta Salad

  1. It's 3 cups of bow-tie pasta, uncooked.
  2. You need 2/3 cup of cheddar cheese crumbles.
  3. It's 6 slices of cooked bacon, diced.
  4. It's 2-3 of small tomatoes, seeded & diced.
  5. You need 2/3 cup of fresh broccoli, chopped.
  6. You need 2/3 cup of red onions, diced.
  7. It's 1/3 cup of black olives.
  8. It's 1/2 cup of mayonnaise.
  9. You need 1/2 cup of creamy ranch dressing.
  10. Prepare 1 clove of garlic, minced.
  11. Prepare 1/2-1 teaspoon of oregano.

Bacon Cheddar Ranch Pasta Salad instructions

  1. Cook pasta until al dente. Be careful not overcook. Drain pasta and rinse with cold water until pasta is cool. Add drained pasta to a large bowl. Add in the bacon, broccoli, red onions, black olives, and tomatoes. essing in with the pasta mixture until well coated..
  2. Toss to combine. (you may need to add a tiny bit of water to moisten the pasta if it's too sticky to toss) In a small bowl, stir together the mayonnaise, ranch dressing, garlic and oregano. When ready to serve, mix the dressing.
  3. Serve immediately..
