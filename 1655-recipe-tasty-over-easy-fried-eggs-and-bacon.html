Over Easy Fried Eggs and Bacon step by step

  1. On medium high in a large skillet cook your favorite bacon to your taste. Remove bacon when done..
  2. Crack the two eggs and place in the bacon grease in the skillet about 2 inches apart..
  3. With a slotted spatula lightly flip the bacon grease over the eggs till you see them turn white 20-30 seconds. Then flip over for 15-to 30 seconds depending on how you like your eggs.
