Ingredients of crock pot chicken parmesan

  1. It's 25 oz of spaghetti sauce.
  2. It's 3 of skinless chicken breasts.
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of italian panko bread crumbs.
  4. You need 1 of egg.
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup of milk.
  6. You need 1/2 tsp of garlic powder.
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp of dried oregano.
  8. You need 1/2 tsp of dried basil.
  9. You need 2 tbsp of oil.
  10. You need 1 cup of shredded mozzarelle cheese.
  11. You need 1 packages of spaghetti.

crock pot chicken parmesan instructions

  1. pour sauce into crock pot.
  2. get two bowls.
  3. in one whisk together egg and milk.
  4. in second bowl combine bread crumbs, garlic powder, oregano, and basil.
  5. in a large skillet heat up vegetable oil.
  6. dip each chicken brest into egg.
  7. roll in bread crumbs.
  8. add to skillet.
  9. brown crusts on both side.
  10. add chicken to crock pot.
  11. cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours.
  12. cook noodles 30 minutes before serving.
  13. 10-15 mibutes before sprinkle with cheese.
  14. serve over spaghetti.
