Ingredients of Chicken Flower Dumpling

  1. You need 400 gms of chicken mince.
  2. Prepare to taste of salt.
  3. You need 1 cup of coriander leaves, chopped.
  4. You need 2 tbsp. of fried onions.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp. of ginger-garlic paste.
  6. You need 1 tsp. of red chilli flakes.
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup of rice, soaked in water for an hour & drained.

Chicken Flower Dumpling instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix together all the above mentioned ingredients (except rice)..
  2. Grease your palm and make small round balls out of the mix. Coat with the soaked rice..
  3. Steam for 15 minutes or till the rice is fully cooked..
  4. Your chicken flower dumplings are ready to be served..
  5. Serve them with any chutney or sauce..
