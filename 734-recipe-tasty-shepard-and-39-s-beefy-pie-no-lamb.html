Shepard's beefy pie (No Lamb) instructions

  1. preheat oven to 350.
  2. make all mashed potatoes.
  3. chop onion.
  4. make both servings of biscuit mix.
  5. add onion to beef.
  6. brown ground beef.
  7. lightly butter bottom of a 7x9 pan.
  8. layer bottom of pan with mashed potatoes.
  9. layer on the ground beef.
  10. add layer of cheese (to desired amount).
  11. add healthy "globs" of uncooked buscuit mix to top.
  12. place into oven for 25 minutes or until "biscuits" are golden-brown.
